Cheesy Broccoli

Description

A divine fusion of vibrant green florets and velvety, melted cheese that takes your taste buds on a captivating journey. The tender, nutrient-packed broccoli mingles harmoniously with a luscious blanket of melted cheese, creating a symphony of flavors that tantalize your senses. With each bite, you experience the delightful contrast of the crisp broccoli texture and the ooey-gooey indulgence of the cheese, transforming a humble vegetable into a truly irresistible delight. Whether enjoyed as a comforting side dish or a satisfying main course, cheesy broccoli is a mouthwatering ode to the perfect marriage of wholesome goodness and irresistible cheesy decadence.

Ingredients

  • Broccoli florets
  • Cheddar cheese (grated)
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • All-purpose flour
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Garlic powder (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and lightly grease a baking dish.
  2. Steam or blanch the broccoli florets until they are crisp-tender. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the flour and whisk continuously for about a minute to form a smooth paste, known as a roux.
  4. Gradually pour in the milk while whisking constantly to prevent lumps from forming. Continue to cook and stir until the mixture thickens and comes to a gentle boil.
  5. Reduce the heat to low and add the grated cheddar cheese. Stir until the cheese melts completely and the sauce becomes smooth and creamy.
  6. Season the cheese sauce with salt, black pepper, and garlic powder (if desired) to taste. Adjust the seasonings according to your preference.
  7. Arrange the steamed broccoli florets in the greased baking dish.
  8. Pour the cheese sauce over the broccoli, ensuring it is evenly coated.
  9. Place the baking dish in the preheated oven and bake for about 15-20 minutes or until the cheese is golden and bubbly.
  10. Remove from the oven and let it cool slightly before serving.
